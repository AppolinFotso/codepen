My Story

I have a true passion for technology. When I was a little boy, I used to repair broken electronic devices especially radios because they were one of the most used and affordable devices at that time. But after obtaining my baccalaureat, I did something different from my passion because of circumstances and reality of life that the majority of people faced in Africa. But my passion is still driving me and I am motivated to learn as much as I can so that I can work as a software developer one day and teach others who are also passionate to learn. I am truly exited about learning how to code and ready to face all the challenges that come with it. I want to join Code Your Future firstly, because I want to become a Professional Web Developer building websites and web applications. Also, I want to help others to understand web technology. Secondly, because Code Your Future boosted my confidence and gave me the opportunity to believe in myself again which is a huge motivation for me to learn more about web technology. Additionally, I would like to become a professional developer because I have passion for technology, I like creativity and I like to positively impact other people lives. When I look at a well designed website or a web application, I often ask myself how creative and fun it must be for someone to have the practical knowledge to build websites or web applications, I want to be that person that is the reason I believe Code Your Future is the right place for me to become a professional developer and hopefully create my own company in the future. Furthermore, I am a hardworking, flexible and adaptable person. I am motivated to complete this course because I have chosen this as my profession and I will put all the effort, time, and energy to achieve my dream profession.
