My Story

I have a true passion for technology. When I was a little boy, I used to try and repair broken electronic devices especially radios because they were the only affordable device at that time. But after obtaining my baccalaureat, I did something different from my passion because of circumstances and reality of life that the majority of people faced in Africa. But my passion is still driving me and I am motivated to learn as much as I can so that I can work as a software developer one day and teach others who are also passionate to learn. I am truly exited about learning how to code and ready to face all the challenges that come with it.
